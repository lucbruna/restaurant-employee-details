## Portfolio Description
Bhukkad is an open-source, free-to-use restaurant operations platform built for modern hospitality teams. The product brings together POS workflows, kitchen order ticketing, menu management, live analytics, reporting, and staff-facing operational tools into one connected system. Instead of locking restaurants into expensive software, Bhukkad is designed as a practical and accessible operating layer that teams can use, adapt, and improve on their own terms. From the floor to the kitchen to the manager dashboard, the platform focuses on reducing service friction, improving coordination, and making everyday restaurant operations faster, clearer, and more reliable.
