01 / Brand core

Bhukkad feels like service rhythm made visible.

Bhukkad is a restaurant operating system that should feel fast, confident, and hospitality-led without drifting into generic SaaS coldness. The brand sits between the urgency of a live service floor and the calm structure that operators need to make decisions quickly.

The visual system should always read globally polished first. Its Indian influence comes through warmth, spice-toned contrast, menu richness, and tactile surfaces rather than overt motifs or themed decoration.

06 / Guardrails

Consistency comes from restraint as much as expression.

Do

  • Use semantic surface tokens instead of raw utility greys.
  • Keep primary orange for action, urgency, and intentional focus.
  • Use larger radii on hero panels, cards, and high-value interactions.
  • Let Sora punctuate brand moments, not dense operational copy.
  • Use freshness accents to stabilize dashboards, not overpower them.

Avoid

  • Enterprise slate chrome with an orange button dropped on top.
  • Random chart colors that have no semantic or service meaning.
  • Overusing gradients, glow, or saturated blocks as decoration.
  • Mixing hard-edged components with the rounded Bhukkad surface system.
  • Turning cultural cues into themed or stereotype-heavy motifs.
